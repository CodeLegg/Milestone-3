Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Marinade:

    In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, sake, mirin, brown sugar, honey, minced garlic, and grated ginger to create the honey garlic marinade.

  2. Marinate the Chicken:

    Cut the chicken thighs into bite-sized pieces. Place the chicken in a resealable plastic bag or a bowl and pour half of the honey garlic marinade over it. Seal the bag or cover the bowl, ensuring the chicken is well-coated. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes (or up to overnight for enhanced flavor).

  3. Cook the Chicken:

    Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade, allowing excess marinade to drip off. Add the chicken to the hot skillet and cook until browned on all sides and cooked through.

  4. Make the Honey Garlic Glaze:

    In a small saucepan, combine the remaining honey garlic marinade with cornstarch and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens. This will be the honey garlic glaze for the chicken.

  5. Glaze and Serve:

    Once the chicken is cooked, pour the honey garlic glaze over it, ensuring each piece is coated evenly. Toss the chicken in the glaze until well-coated and heated through. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions if desired.

Cooking Tip:

For an extra burst of flavor, consider marinating the chicken in the honey garlic marinade for an extended period. Allowing the chicken to marinate overnight in the refrigerator enhances its tenderness and infuses it with the rich flavors of garlic, honey, and spices. This simple step ensures a more succulent and flavorful Honey Garlic Chicken experience when you cook it.
