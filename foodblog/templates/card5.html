Ingredients:

  • 3 envelopes unflavored gelatin
  • 1 cup cold water, divided
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light corn syrup
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon mint extract
  • Powdered sugar for dusting
  • Mini chocolate chips or dark chocolate, melted
  • Small pretzel sticks
  • Edible candy eyes
  • Peppermint candies or small round candies for buttons
  • Orange icing or small pieces of orange candy for the carrot nose

Instructions:


  1. Prepare Marshmallow Mixture:

    In a bowl, combine gelatin with 1/2 cup of cold water. Let it bloom while preparing the sugar syrup. In a saucepan, combine granulated sugar, corn syrup, and the remaining 1/2 cup of water. Heat until the sugar dissolves. Pour over the bloomed gelatin, add salt and mint extract, then beat until thick and fluffy with an electric mixer.

  2. Shape the Marshmallows:

    Dust a flat surface with powdered sugar. Transfer the marshmallow mixture, spread into an even layer, and use snowman-shaped cookie cutters or a knife to cut out marshmallow snowmen.

  3. Decorate the Snowmen:

    Melt dark chocolate or use mini chocolate chips for eyes and buttons. Attach them along with broken pretzel sticks for arms, edible candy eyes, peppermint candies for buttons, and orange icing or candy for the carrot nose.

  4. Let Them Set:

    Allow the marshmallow snowmen to set for a few hours or overnight. Once set, carefully remove them from the powdered sugar surface.

Cooking Tip:

When creating Mint Snowman Marshmallows, precision in measuring ingredients and timing is crucial. Follow the recipe meticulously, ensuring accurate measurements for ingredients like gelatin, sugar, and mint extract. Additionally, pay close attention to the timing during each step, especially when heating the sugar syrup and beating the marshmallow mixture.

Precision ensures that the marshmallows set correctly, achieving the ideal texture and flavor. It's the key to crafting snowmen that are not only visually appealing but also delightfully fluffy and minty. Take your time to measure accurately and monitor temperatures closely, and your Mint Snowman Marshmallows will be a masterpiece of both taste and presentation.
