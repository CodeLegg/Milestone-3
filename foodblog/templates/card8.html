Ingredients:

  • 1 spiral-sliced ham (7-9 pounds)
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup whole grain mustard
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 325째F (163째C).

  2. Prepare the Ham:

    Place the spiral-sliced ham in a roasting pan, cut side down.

  3. Mix the Glaze:

    In a saucepan, combine honey, Dijon mustard, brown sugar, orange juice, whole grain mustard, ground cinnamon, ground cloves, and ground nutmeg. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture is well combined and slightly thickened.

  4. Glaze the Ham:

    Brush a generous amount of the glaze over the entire surface of the ham, ensuring it gets into the slices.

  5. Bake:

    Cover the ham with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 1 hour, occasionally basting with the glaze. Remove the foil for the last 15-20 minutes to allow the ham to caramelize.

  6. Check Internal Temperature:

    Ensure the ham reaches an internal temperature of 140째F (60째C). If needed, adjust the baking time accordingly.

  7. Rest and Serve:

    Allow the ham to rest for 10-15 minutes before carving. Serve slices with the remaining glaze on the side.

Cooking Tip:

For an extra layer of flavor and moisture, consider scoring the surface of the ham in a crisscross pattern before applying the glaze. This not only enhances the presentation by creating an appealing diamond pattern but also allows the glaze to penetrate deeper, infusing the ham with a perfect balance of sweetness and savory goodness. Additionally, baste the ham with the pan juices throughout the cooking process to ensure a succulent and flavorful outcome. Enjoy the delightful combination of a caramelized exterior and juicy interior for a memorable Honey Baked Ham experience!
