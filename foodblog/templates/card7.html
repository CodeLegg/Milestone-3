Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (240g) high-quality milk chocolate, chopped
  • Hot cocoa mix (about 4 tablespoons)
  • Mini marshmallows (as needed)
  • Sprinkles or crushed candy canes (optional, for decoration)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Chocolate Shells:

    Temper the chopped milk chocolate. Spoon a tablespoon of melted chocolate into each cavity of a spherical silicone mold. Use the back of the spoon to spread the chocolate, ensuring full coverage. Chill in the refrigerator until set.

  2. Add Hot Cocoa Mix and Marshmallows:

    Remove the chocolate shells from the mold. Fill half of them with hot cocoa mix and mini marshmallows. Optionally, add sprinkles or crushed candy canes for extra flavor and decoration.

  3. Seal the Hot Chocolate Bombs:

    Heat a plate or a flat surface, then briefly press each empty chocolate shell onto the warm surface to melt the edges. Immediately place it onto a filled shell, sealing the two halves together. This creates a sphere with hot cocoa mix and marshmallows inside.

  4. Decorate (Optional):

    Drizzle the hot chocolate bombs with additional melted chocolate and decorate with more sprinkles or crushed candy canes if desired.

  5. Serve:

    Place a hot chocolate bomb in a mug. Pour hot milk over it, and watch as the chocolate shell melts, releasing the cocoa mix and marshmallows. Stir until well combined and enjoy your delightful hot chocolate!
