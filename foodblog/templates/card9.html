Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk (110°F/43°C)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • For the Filling:
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • For the Cream Cheese Frosting:
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast:

    In a bowl, combine warm milk, melted butter, and granulated sugar. Sprinkle the yeast over the mixture, stir, and let it sit for about 5 minutes until foamy.

  2. Prepare the Dough:

    In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg. Pour in the yeast mixture and mix until a soft dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth, then place it in a greased bowl. Cover and let it rise until doubled in size, usually about 1 hour.

  3. Roll and Fill:

    Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface into a rectangle. Spread softened butter over the surface and sprinkle brown sugar and cinnamon evenly. Roll the dough tightly from the longer side and cut into individual rolls.

  4. Bake:

    Place the rolls in a greased baking pan, cover, and let them rise for another 30 minutes. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.

  5. Make the Cream Cheese Frosting:

    While the rolls are baking, prepare the cream cheese frosting. Beat together softened cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  6. Frost and Enjoy:

    Once the cinnamon rolls are out of the oven, let them cool slightly before generously spreading the cream cheese frosting over the top. Serve warm and indulge in the heavenly aroma and taste of homemade cinnamon rolls.
